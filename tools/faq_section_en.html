Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes, the Free (Basic) plan is completely free. It is funded by tasteful, unobtrusive ads in the admin panel. There are no hidden fees, and you never need to cancel because we will never charge you for the basic plan.

Includes: Reservation management, rooms, data export, admin panel and mobile app (coming soon).

Not included: The digital check-in module (submission to the Spanish Ministry of the Interior), which is always priced at €8/month.

You never need to enter a credit card for the basic plan—no tricks or fine print.

Is it free forever?

The Basic plan is designed to be free of monthly charges for users who register during the launch phase, subject to the terms of service and keeping the account active.

As long as your account stays active, we will not charge a monthly fee for the basic plan. It is funded by discreet ads in the panel.

Important: Features included in the plan may evolve with the product, but we will always keep a free plan with no monthly fee.

No, you do not need a credit card for the Free (Basic) plan.

If you registered early, you keep permanent access to the Free (Basic) plan with no monthly fee.

How does the digital check-in module work?

The digital check-in module lets guests complete their details before arrival and automatically submits records to the Spanish Ministry of the Interior under current rules (RD 933/2021).

This module always costs €8 per month, with no exceptions. It includes:
• Digital check-in form for guests
• Automatic submission to the Ministry of the Interior
• Compliance-focused workflow
• No ads in the panel
• Priority support

How does online check-in work?

Copy the link from the “Registrations” or “Forms” section of your dashboard and send it to guests. You can add it to Airbnb or Booking message templates so guests receive it automatically when a booking is made. You only confirm receipt and then send your check-in instructions and house rules.

The traveller completes their data, uploads documents and signs if needed, in their own language. On arrival, you mainly verify identity and hand over keys.

Can I export data for my accountant and tax return?

Yes. You can download a single CSV/Excel file with revenue, fees and occupancy filtered by date range or tax year. The PMS includes full reservation and room management at no charge on the free tier.

How does traveller registration work?

Traveller registration with automatic Ministry submission is available on the Check-in plan (from €8/month). There you can confirm that the guest has completed the data and that both the Traveller Report (PV) and Lodging Reservation (RH) have been sent to the Spanish Ministry of the Interior—required for short-stay accommodation in Spain (RD 933/2021).

What languages does the system support?
